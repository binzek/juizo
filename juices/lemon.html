Lemon Juice

Refresh your energy with fresh make lemon juice. Yay! it is easy to make

- Suggestion by someone

Credits

Ingredients

  • A piece of lemon
  • A glass of water
  • A teaspoon sugar

Method

  1. Take the glass of water.
  2. Squeeze lemon into it.
  3. Add sugar into glass.
  4. Stir and serve.
